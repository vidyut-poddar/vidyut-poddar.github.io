My career started in B2B manufacturing (Satibox), moved into B2C Fintech support at Juno — where I learned what it really means to own metrics like FRT, AHT, and CSAT under volume — and now I spend my days at HighLevel, an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform serving agencies.

At HighLevel I was hired as a Customer Support Representative in October 2023. A year later I was promoted to Account Specialist, where I now own a portfolio of high-value agency accounts. My job, on paper, is to keep those accounts healthy. In practice, my job is whatever has to happen for those accounts to keep winning — and that has meant a lot of things.

I've manually restored 700+ deleted sub-accounts in a week when an engineering fix wasn't coming. I've written JavaScript dashboards because the product didn't have the analytics a client needed. I've designed an automated A2P email-parser workflow that syncs to Google Sheets because agencies needed real-time status visibility. I've stood up Q&A bots, talked clients through SIP trunking, and built a custom Voice-AI-to-contact-note pipeline because the standard voicemail flow wasn't working.

What I've learned: the line between "support," "success," "solutions engineering," and "product" is artificial. Customers don't care which team you sit on. They care whether you can ship a fix. That's the job I've grown into, and that's the job I love.
